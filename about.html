Our Journey

From Vision to Reality

TiC Foundation was born in 2020 from a simple yet powerful observation: young people in underserved communities possess incredible potential, but often lack access to the resources, mentorship, and opportunities needed to transform their ideas into reality.

Our founder, an individual, passionate technologists and community leader, recognized that the digital divide wasn't just about access to technology—it was about access to opportunity, education, and the networks that enable innovation to flourish.

What started as a small coding bootcamp in a community center has grown into a comprehensive ecosystem of programs that address the multifaceted challenges facing today's youth while preparing them to be tomorrow's leaders.

Mission, Vision & Values

Our guiding principles shape everything we do and drive our commitment to creating lasting change in communities worldwide.

Our Vision

A world where every young person, regardless of their background, has access to the tools, knowledge, and opportunities needed to become an agent of positive transformation in their community.

Our Impact

Our Journey

Timeline of Impact

From our humble beginnings to becoming a catalyst for community transformation, here's how we've grown and evolved.

Foundation Launch

2020

TiC Foundation was officially established with a mission to bridge the digital divide. We started with a small team of passionate individuals and our first coding bootcamp serving 25 youth in our local community.

First Major Milestone

2021

Reached our first 100 program participants and launched our entrepreneurship track. Established partnerships with 3 local schools and received our first major grant funding of $50,000.

Regional Expansion

2022

Expanded operations to 10 communities across the region. Launched our Innovation Labs program and established a network of 50+ volunteer mentors. Total participants reached 500+.

Innovation & Growth

2023

Launched our flagship Innovation Labs in 5 major cities. Introduced AI/ML curriculum and established our first startup incubator. 15 youth-led startups received seed funding.

Transformative Impact

2024

Celebrated our 1000th program graduate and expanded to 25 communities. Launched our digital platform reaching youth globally and established partnerships with major tech companies for internship programs.
